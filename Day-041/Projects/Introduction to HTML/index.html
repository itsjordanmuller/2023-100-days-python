The Best Movies According to Jordan

My top 3 movies of all-time

2001: A Space Odyssey

Stanley Kubrick's masterpiece offers a surreal journey through space and time. Its visionary visuals, pioneering special effects, and profound themes about human evolution and artificial intelligence have left an indelible mark on cinematic history.

Interstellar

Christopher Nolan's space epic combines breathtaking visuals with a deeply emotional human story. Its ambitious narrative delves into complex scientific concepts while offering a touching exploration of love, sacrifice, and the bond between parent and child.

Arrival

Dennis Villeneuve's thought-provoking film is a unique take on alien encounters. With its masterful storytelling and intriguing linguistic concepts, it challenges our perceptions of time, communication, and humanity's place in the universe.
